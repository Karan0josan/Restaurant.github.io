About Us

Wok of China

Welcome to Wok of China! Our mission is to provide our customers with authentic Chinese cuisine that is made with the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking techniques. We strive to create an environment that is warm, welcoming, and relaxing, so you can enjoy your dining experience to the fullest.

We offer a variety of dishes that are inspired by the diverse regions of China, from spicy Sichuan cuisine to delicate Cantonese dishes. Our menu features everything from classic favorites like Kung Pao Chicken and Beef and Broccoli to more exotic dishes like Hot and Sour Soup and Ma Po Tofu. We also offer a selection of vegetarian and gluten-free options.

At our restaurant, we believe in providing excellent service to our customers. Our staff is friendly, knowledgeable, and always ready to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have. We also offer takeout and delivery services for your convenience.

We look forward to serving you and introducing you to the wonderful world of Chinese cuisine!
